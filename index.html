Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Information We Collect

Paper Page does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. Tasks, notes and other information created in the app are stored locally on your device.

The app does not use analytics, advertising networks, tracking technologies, or profiling.

All content created by the user remains on the user’s device unless the user chooses to back up or transfer their device using Apple services.
